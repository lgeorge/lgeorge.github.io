FPMT Basic Program - Online Courses

This information is for students interested in studying the FPMT Basic Program ("BP") through online courses being offered in North America. For general information about the program, visit the FPMT Basic Program page, or download a list of Basic Program subjects and associated texts.

To register interest in future course offerings, add your contact info here. To add a new course to this list, fill out this form.

In the following list, an "O" in the the "Open/Closed" column means that a student can attend one or more individual sessions; "C" means the course is closed to new students after it starts. "Cert" means whether completion of the course fulfills the Basic Program requirement for the subject.

  1. Stages of the Path. Stages of the Path to Enlightenment of beings of low, middling and great capacity.

  2. Heart Sutra. Emptiness and the phenomena qualified by it.

  3. Mahayana Mind training. The law of actions and results, or karma. The states of mind that shape future experiences.

  4. Engaging in the Bodhisattva Deeds. The Six Perfections and how to practice them.

  5. Mind and Cognition. The way the mind knows phenomena and mental factors that constitute the basis of experience.

  6. Tenets. The philosophical systems, or tenets, of the four main schools of ancient Buddhist thought.

  7. Ornament for Clear Realization. The Path to Buddhahood.

            No classes currently offered.

  8. The Tathagata Essence. The Buddha lineage that exists within every sentient being.

            No classes currently offered.

  9. Grounds and Paths of Secret Mantra & The Three Basic Bodies. The structure of the path of Tantra.

            No classes currently offered.
